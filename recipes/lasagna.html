Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a type of pasta made in very wide flat sheets. It's a Italian dish made in the 14th century. It's basically stacked layers of pasta alternating with fillings.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
  7. Enjoy!

How to layer the lasagna:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
