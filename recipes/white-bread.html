White Bread for the Bread Machine

Description:

White breads are breads made from wheat flour, which the bran and the germ layers have been removed from the whole wheat berry. This version is made on the machine, making the process easier.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package bread machine yeast
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Place water, sugar and yeast in the pan of the bread machine
  2. Let yeast dissolve and foam for 10 minutes
  3. Add oil, flour and salt to the yeast
  4. Select Basic or White Bread setting, and press Start
  5. Remove loaf from the machine after the cycle is done
  6. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes before turning it out onto a rack to cool completely
  7. Enjoy!
